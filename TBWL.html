THE BOY WHO LOVED

ScribblingQuills

Blurb

The Boy Who Loved is a fan made book made similar to the original Harry Potter series but in there is another character. Y/n(your name) Scamander daughter of Sean and Alexis Scamander catches the eye of The Boy Who Lived(Harry Potter)

Review

ScribblingQuills did a great job making this book, this is not like the original series she added her own element to this book that made it unique, if youre a Harry lover or just love Harry Potter I strongly recommend this book
