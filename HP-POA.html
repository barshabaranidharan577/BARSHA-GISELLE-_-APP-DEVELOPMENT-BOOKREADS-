HARRY POTTER AND THE PRISONER OF AZKABAN

J.K ROWLING

Blurb

In Harry’s third year a mass murderer and one of Voldermort’s allies by the name of Sirius Black has escaped Azkaban(wizard prison) and is ought to get Harry and possibly murder him?

Review

Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban is probably one of my favourite Harry Potter books it shows how much every character has grown up and matured, the plot twist in the end is unexpected. I strongly recommend this book but read the pervious ones first
